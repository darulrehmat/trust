ABOUT US

Established in 2008, Dar-Ul-Rehmat Trust is a charitable healthcare organization founded on the vision of Mian Allah Wasia (late), who generously donated the land for its creation. We provide essential medical care to approximately 200 patients daily, treating a variety of conditions including general medicine, pediatrics, and gynecology.

Our dedicated team offers free or affordable healthcare to underserved communities, ensuring no one is turned away due to financial constraints. Committed to compassion and service, we continue to uphold our founder's mission of making healthcare accessible to all.
